How it works

From sign-up to selling in four steps.

  1. 1

    Set up your pipeline

    Define your stages, import leads and customers, and shape the deal flow to match how you sell.

  2. 2

    Plan & log activity

    Reps plan visits and calls, then log activity from the field on native mobile apps.

  3. 3

    Let AI prioritise

    Deal-probability scoring surfaces the deals most likely to close, so effort goes where it counts.

  4. 4

    Manage in real time

    Managers track targets and pipeline health with real-time analytics — no end-of-month surprises.
